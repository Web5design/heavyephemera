A turing complete sequencer.

Wiresq is based on Wireworld, a cellular automata which can exhibit the features of digital logic circuits easily and compactly. Compared with the game of life, drawing a memory circuit is quite easy.

You can construct systems within wiresq that display complex but repeatable behaivor, and experiment with circuit design.

A simple synthesizer.

A three waveform subtractive synthesizer with an agressive filter. Enough to sketch out your ideas, not too much to get bogged down in.
