Editor

World

Wires are yellow. Sparks are red. Tails are blue.

You can toggle between painting cells and scrolling by clicking the icon in the top left corner.

Rules

Sparks always become Tails.

Tails always become Wires.

Wires become Sparks if one or two of the neighbor cells are Sparks. The neighbors can be either right next door or on a diagonal.

Links

Drag from the synth in the left column to a cell in the world. Double click a cell to remove an existing link for the selected synth.

Notes

Double tap a cell to bring up the note selector. Any synths linked to this cell will trigger with this note. Tap outside the selector to hide it.

Speed

Tap the speed in the top bar to bring up the speed control. There is also a clock multiplyer to simplify building structures that need to run faster.

Synths

This is a simple subtractive synth. There are controls for the volume envelope (ATK, HLD, REL), the waveform, and a filter (CUT, RES).

Structures

Clock

Any closed loop will create a clock. You can generate pulses at different rates by changing the size of the loops.

Diode

You can control the direction of sparks by placing diodes. This one will only allow sparks to go downward, not upwards.

XOR

A spark coming from the left or right will pass, but if two sparks arrive simultaneously, neither will pass. Make sure your timing is right on this or you may have strage sparks traveling back up your wires.

AND-NOT

A spark coming from the left will pass unless a spark arrives simultaneously from the right. A spark from the right will never pass.
